About Us

‘SMART DILUENTS & LAB DEVICES’ is a supplying firm, that supplies you all kind of laboratory chemicals and consumables required for your routine analysis, study, analytical development, research & company / school projects. We are dealing in all type of laboratory chemicals & laboratory consumables from a range of top laboratory brands.

We will providing different grade of lab chemicals, solvents, reference / working standards, impurity standard, lab apparatus of Class-A, HPLC-GC consumables & other consumables for you at one spot .

We will fulfill all your lab needs with competent and attractive rates which allow you to do hassle free procurement. ‘SMART DILUENTS & LAB DEVICES’ deals with different manufacturer brands and do vital comparison for you and providing comprehensive deal for your laboratory. Our experienced team gives you a sustainable solution. As we are a multi brand holders in all segment which shall limited your procurement task from multiple window to single window which minimizes your valuable time of procurement .
